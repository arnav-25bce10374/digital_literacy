# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

###  Do’s
- Use strong passwords  
- Keep your profiles professional  
- Verify the content you share or re-share  
- Respect the opinions of others and communicate accordingly  
- Adjust the privacy settings on your social media accounts  

###  Don’ts
- Don’t share personal content or sensitive information on social media  
- Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content on social media  
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or arguments on social media  
- Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people on social media  
- Don’t over-share your daily activities or location on social media
