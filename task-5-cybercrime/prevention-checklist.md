## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown email attachments  
- Never share personal information like OTP, passwords, or bank details  
- Verify the identity of people before trusting messages or calls  

###  UPI / Financial Safety
- Never approve unknown payment requests or scan random QR codes  
- Double-check the name of the person before sending money  

- Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security vulnerabilities  
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  

###  Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
